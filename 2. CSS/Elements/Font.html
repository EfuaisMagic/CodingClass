Every font belongs to one of the five Font Families
of which sans-serif and serif are the most commonly used. The name of the family should be added at the very end of every font-family: font, font-font, family property and will be applied if the font fails to display.

sans-serif (no stroked, tapered or flared ends)

  • Verdana:sans-serif;
  • Tahoma:sans-serif;
  • Trebuchet MS:sans-serif;
  • Comic Sans MS:sans-serif;
  • Arial:sans-serif;
